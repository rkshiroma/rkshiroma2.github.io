Occupational
Therapy Services

Bright Beginnings for Kids, Inc. provides outpatient pediatric occupational therapy services (OT) for children with various diagnosis. In addition to OT, educational therapy services are also offered.

Explore our services & inquire about
occupational therapy services today.

Areas We Treat:

- Children with developmental delays
- Feeding difficulties
- Sensory processing difficulties
- Educational difficulties
- Decreased processing skills
- Early adulthood
- Decreased executive function skills

Services:

- 15 minute initial consultation
- Evaluation and written report
- Developmental consultation
- Private sessions
- Sibling sessions
- Dual session
